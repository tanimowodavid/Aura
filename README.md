# Aura – AI Financial Check‑In Assistant

## 1. What Aura Is

Aura is a **voice‑ or chat‑based AI financial check‑in assistant** designed for people who already earn money and want calm structure, accountability, and clarity around their finances.

Aura does **not** replace a human financial advisor. Aura acts as a **reflection and accountability companion** that helps users:

- Understand where their money is going
- Stay aligned with a self‑defined financial goal
- Build better money habits without stress or bank integrations
